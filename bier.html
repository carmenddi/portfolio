LOGO BREWERY

For the brewery The Golden Hop I created the bottle label, and the cap. Hops are one of the main ingredients for brewing and are represented here on both the label and the cap. The design of this product wants to communicate essentiality and the name hints at high quality. Golden in colour, only the word 'Hop' to recall the adjective 'Golden'. For the opening of the pub upstairs, the design was also extended to the glasses with slight modifications. Both the font and the outline of the hops remain the same, but the positioning changes. The lettering here is on two levels, the hop figures are two and enclose the word 'Hop'. In addition to glasses, this design is also intended for menus, napkins or to be applied on the window display.
