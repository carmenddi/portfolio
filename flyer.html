FLYERS RESTAURANT

For the Flyers to promote the opening of the Halfmoon restaurant, I chose to go for something very simple, but effective. Since the first impact is important, the focus is on photos that are meant to summarise the restaurant's philosophy. The imperfection of the handmade fresh pasta summarises freshness and craftsmanship, the colours of the dish with the fish represent extroversion, but also balance, and each individual ingredient, which comes from a certain cooking process, represents complexity, and finally the dessert, with its light colours and linearity represents elegance and simplicity. Obviously, the intention is not just to attract the attention of a potential customer, so the insert on the right-hand side summarises the important information to be communicated and thus the purpose for which the flyer was created. PS: the restaurant does not really exist.
