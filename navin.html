WEBSITE GRAPHIC

To mark the launch of the app, the start-up Nav-in decided to make a small change to the website. As soon as a user opens it, they will see that the app is finally available. Starting with a 2D design, in which a phone is visible with the app open and the route signposted, we arrive at the final graphics, in which the app 'comes to life', leaves the phone and becomes three-dimensional. The simplicity and monotony of two dimensions is transformed into dynamism and movement. On the side are the steps to follow to get the app.
