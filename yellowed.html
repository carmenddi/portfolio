YELLOWED LOGO

Yellowed is a small business dedicated to selling vintage advertisements. The name is reminiscent of the yellowed pages of old newspapers and the logo picks up on the colour yellow, which becomes the main colour. The lettering is framed in two squares and even here the choice is not casual, in fact the purpose of the business is the sale of framed advertisements, conceived as distinctive decorative elements.
