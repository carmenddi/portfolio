YOGA STUDIO

For the creation of this logo for a yoga studio, I focused on a design that was essential, elegant and gave an idea of lightness. For the design, I thought of giving harmony by using the golden ratio to define and soften the lines. The two meeting hands are meant to resemble the letter Y in reverse. The logo appears on business cards and t-shirts.
